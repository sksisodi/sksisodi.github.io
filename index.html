Work

The Alchemist

'The Alchemist' is a game project dedicated to studying the effects of story progression on user engagement and perception of narrative.

Shibu

'Shibu' is an innovative role-playing game designed to foster the use of gender-neutral pronouns.

Resilience

StudyCrafter
