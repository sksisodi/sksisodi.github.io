Work

The Alchemist

'The Alchemist' is a game project dedicated to studying the effects of story progression on user engagement and perception of narrative.

Game Mechanics

The project explores application of cluster analysis to qualitative data, bridging the gap between quantitative and qualitative methodologies.

Resilience

'The Lux', is an augmented reality game designed to study the resilience of first-year students at UCSC.

Pseudo-Scientist

'Pseudo-Scientist', a multiform Twine story that intertwines relatable narratives and design to study Impostor Syndrome.

StudyCrafter

'StudyCrafter' is a project that explores the use of AI to integrate into a gamified study design platform.

Shibu

'Shibu' is an innovative role-playing game designed to foster the use of gender-neutral pronouns.
