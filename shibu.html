Shibu

Shibu is an innovative role-playing game (RPG) designed to foster an understanding and practice of gender-inclusive pronouns. This project inspired by my own journey and challenges as a non-native English speaker. It aims to address the nuances of gender identity through interactive gameplay, emphasizing the importance of mindful communication.

My Role
Project Lead
Team Size
3
Timeline
10 weeks
Tools Used
Figma,

In the heart of Shibu's development, we embraced an iterative process, rigorously researching and brainstorming to shape a game that is both captivating and educational. The game narrative invites players to step into the role of a novice reporter, navigating through stories of superhuman named "Shibu". Players engage in third-person conversations, making choices that shape their understanding of gender pronouns. This journey through Shibu's world is a delicate balance of learning and play, leveraging interactive storytelling to make a profound impact on players' language use.

As a lead, my contributions to Shibu were multifaceted. I played a pivotal role in shaping the project's vision, from initial concept to gameplay mechanics. I also engaged in crafting the narrative, ensuring it remained sensitive to the themes of gender identity and inclusivity.
